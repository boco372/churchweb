Kindergarten – Little Cubs Many children recently have turned five and started school. This means the kindergarten has spaces and needs more enrolments. Please pray for more children.

Feedback please: The Leadership Team is considering the issue of ground cover for the ‘garden’ the Kindergarten play area, currently covered by black matting, around ‘Beryl’s Tree.
We have several factors at play: 1. Beryl’s Tree continues to grow and the roots have lifted the matting to a stage where it is becoming a safety issue for anyone playing on it. The Ministry of Education (Early Childhood) have said the matting must be repaired / replaced.
2. There is a drain running directly under the playground / tree which already we have had to cut root damage from. 3. With the heavy rain / downpours we have been experiencing the drainage flow is not as fast as is needed and water is very close to flowing into the ground floor rooms, of course once the rain stops it clears within minutes.
So the Leadership Team will need to make some decisions all of which will cost, but we have included this in our current budget. Our options are many but the following are our attempts to balance our needs and listen to the Kindergarten’s concerns.
A) We can install a new drain along the concrete / matting edge, and then repair the matting or replace the matting with Tiger Turf like grass.
B) We can install the new drain, replace matting and re-contour the land shape / water flow.
C) We can cut down Beryl’s tree, re-contour the area, install a shade cloth and depending on finances repair matting or install new surface coverings.
We are not asking you to make the decision – but we do want to receive feedback on your thoughts, especially with option (C) – and the removal of Beryl’s Tree. Please talk to Lorraine, Alan or Bob. Thank you.

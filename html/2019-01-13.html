15 The people were waiting expectantly and were all wondering in their hearts if John might possibly be the Messiah. 16 John answered them all, “I baptize you with water. Ministerial Vacancy, the First Step. The Leadership Team has determined that our Coordinating Partner (to be called Appointing Partner after July 2019) from now on is the Anglican Church.
Archdeacon Michael Berry has informed us that Archdeacon Carole Hughes is going to manage this process as all the current vacancies in this diocese are in Michael’s region and he needs to share the workload. They are working on identifying a priest for the interim and will inform us as soon as they can. It is standard Anglican practice to do nothing in a parish until the incumbent has actually left.

Kindergarten playground update Please go and have a look at the children’s playground downstairs. We have had the black matting re-covered with artificial grass. We have had a drain installed along the edge of the concrete pathway. The fixed climbing equipment has been removed and a seating area has been created with bark ground cover of 4.3 by 4.4m around the base of Beryl’s Tree.

We have had a tiler install splashback tiles around the stoves. Thank you Felicity for the donation of the tiles. The light brown plates are too big for the bottom drawer of the dishwasher, so we are replacing them.
